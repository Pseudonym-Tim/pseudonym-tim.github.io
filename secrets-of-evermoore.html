Secrets Of Evermoore

Originally dubbed "EXCAVATOR" in it's pre-alpha phase, Secrets Of Evermoore is a retro point-and-click dungeon crawler FPS that takes you on a journey through ancient subterranean labyrinths.
After becoming separated from your archaeological dig team, it's up to you to uncover the hidden secrets and make it out alive.

Engine: Unity C#

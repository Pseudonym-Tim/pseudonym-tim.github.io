POSTAL: Onslaught

Navigate randomly generated levels, collect an arsenal of weapons and items, and unleash mayhem!
With a score multiplier tied to your killstreak, your main challenge is to keep the combo alive and rack up the highest score, just like the good old days at the arcade!

How long can you survive the pixelated carnage?

This game was made in a relatively short period of time for the 2024 POSTAL game jam.
Inspired by Nuclear Throne and arcade games like Robotron: 2084

Engine: Unity C#
Status: RELEASED!

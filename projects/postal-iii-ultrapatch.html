Postal III Ultrapatch

 Postal III Ultrapatch is a mod for Postal III that addresses a significant number of bugs and crashes that were present in Postal III. Ultrapatch also includes additional improvements, changes, and restored cut content in order to greatly improve the gameplay.

I'm the Lead Programmer working on Postal III Ultrapatch for @Whackjob Interactive

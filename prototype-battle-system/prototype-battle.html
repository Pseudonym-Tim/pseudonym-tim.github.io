Battle System Info

Battles use a shared Active Time Battle (ATB) bar!
Choose what action to perform based on the current fill of the bar and the matching action window it is inside
Each action can only be used once per cycle but when the bar reaches 100% it resets
You may chose to skip an action if you wish, but you will not gain any AP unless you commit
Your remaining unspent Action Points (AP) carry over after ATB resets,
so you can save up to perform stronger moves if you think you can get away with it!


DEFEND (0-25%) Reduces damage of next incoming hit until you act again
ATTACK (25-50%) Gain AP and use basic attacks
SKILL (50-75%) Gain more AP and use multi-hit skills
MAGIC (75-100%) Gain the most AP and cast powerful magic spells

Enemy Behavior

The enemy follows the same ATB rules as you, sometimes skipping actions to stay unpredictable...
When your HP is low, it favors stronger attacks and can also defend to reduce the damage your next hit...

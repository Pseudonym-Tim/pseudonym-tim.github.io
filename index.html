Welcome to my website! I'm a self-taught programmer, modder, and indie game developer.
I also dabble in art and music from time to time, even if I don't consider myself particularly skilled in those areas.
I love writing code and fixing things, and I have a keen interest in computer hardware and electrical engineering as well.
Most of my time is spent working on games, mods, tools, and small experiments just for fun.
I'm currently a member of Whackjob Interactive, an indie development team made up of people from all around the world!
